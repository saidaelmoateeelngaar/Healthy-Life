Nutrition (التغذية)

Healthy food habits for a balanced life

Proteins

Essential for building muscles and repairing tissues.

Include sources like chicken, fish, eggs, beans, and nuts in your daily meals.

Carbohydrates

Main source of energy for your body and brain.

Choose whole grains like oats, rice, and whole wheat bread for sustained energy.

Vitamins & Minerals

Keep your immune system strong and body healthy.

Eat colorful fruits and vegetables to get a variety of vitamins and minerals.

Fruits & Vegetables

Provide fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants.

Aim for at least 5 servings per day, fresh or lightly cooked.

Water

Stay hydrated to maintain energy and focus.

Drink 6–8 glasses daily and more if you exercise or it’s hot.

Healthy Fats

Important for brain function and hormone balance.

Include olive oil, avocado, nuts, and seeds in moderation.

Healthy nutrition is the foundation of a healthy life, and your daily food choices are the key to a stronger body and a better life.
